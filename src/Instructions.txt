Instructions:

For questions that give multiple choice answers, please choose one of the options and only input the number of that option.
For questions that require you to input a word, phrase, name, etc. DO NOT input special characters(only letters).
There will be a score counter in the background. The initial score will be 0.
For every correct answer, the score will be increased by 1, but for every wrong answer, the score will be decreased by 1.
You will not be able to see your score in the middle of the quiz. The final score will be displayed at the end of the quiz.
